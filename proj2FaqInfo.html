What is LB Coats all about?

LB COATS
We offer custom paint jobs and finishes on most vehicles. Please e-mail or contact us to let us know what we can offer for you. We can give you the best and most sought after colors, the best finishes of Gloss, Matte, and Candy paint. We also do single panel jobs if that is what you need.
Our team can provide Wrap, Hydro dipped, Plasti dipped, and paint correction and repairs. We offer a wide variety of paint. Please bring in a sample of your paint that you would like to be applied or choose from our samples that we have here at the shop. No color is unobtainable here at LB Coats.
So let us get your vehicle to where you want to be with our custom one of a kind paint jobs!

F.A.Q

Q:How much are the paint jobs?
A:For candy coats the estimate is $1,200 for a sedan and coupe. Suvs and larger vehicles it will be an additional $1,000. Smaller vehicles such as motorcycles for complete fairing paint jobs in candy coat it will be $1,000. Matte finishes will start at $1,300 with an addition of $800 for larger vehicles. For motorcycles it will be $1,000. Chameleon prices start at $2,500 with an addtional $1,000 for larger vehicles. For motorcycles it will be $1,200. Of course these are all estimates for work. So please come on down or contact us for a precise estimate.

Q:How long will it take for the job to be completed?
A:It usually takes about 2-3 weeks for small vehicles. 3-6 weeks for large vehicles. Motrocycles about 3-4 weeks.

Q:Is there a deposit I need to put down?
A:Yes, We require a 15% NON-REFUNDABLE deposit of an estimated price to begin work.

Q:Do you offer financing?
A:Yes! we offer finacing through major banks and third party lenders.

Q:Can I get something else painted? Such as a Boat, RV, Trailer, or Plane?
A:Yes! We offer services for those vehicles and equipment as well. Contact us for estimates and finish suggestions!

Q:Do y'all do alternative work besides paint jobs?
A:We offer alternative methods to get your ride looking great! such as plasti-dip, hyro-dip, 3M wraps, and single panel work.
